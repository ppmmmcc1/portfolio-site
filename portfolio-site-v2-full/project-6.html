ABOUT

Carbon Filter Fume Extractor

Inspiration

I needed a way to safely solder indoors and decided to create a compact, carbon-filtered extractor from scratch.

Challenges

Maximizing airflow without increasing noise or losing too much suction power was a challenge with 3D printed parts.

Conclusion

The unit is modular, quiet, and effectively filters fumes for soldering and other light lab work.
