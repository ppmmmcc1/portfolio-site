ABOUT

Filament Recycler (Low-Maintenance)

Inspiration

I noticed the lack of affordable, beginner-friendly filament recyclers and wanted to close that gap.

Challenges

Balancing shredder strength, extrusion temperature, and reliable cooling required iterative prototyping.

Conclusion

The final design is compact, easy to use, and can produce 1.75 mm filament from a variety of waste prints with minimal cleaning.
